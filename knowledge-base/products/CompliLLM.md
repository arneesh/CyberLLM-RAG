# CompliLLM

## Compliance Automation Platform

CompliLLM is CyberLLM's AI-powered compliance automation platform, built to eliminate the manual burden of achieving and maintaining compliance with the security and privacy frameworks that govern modern enterprises. From SOC 2 Type II to HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, NIST 800-53, and FedRAMP, CompliLLM continuously monitors an organization's control environment, automatically collects audit evidence, identifies gaps before auditors do, and generates the reports that make audit season painless.

Compliance is traditionally a labor-intensive, point-in-time exercise — organizations scramble for weeks before an audit, manually gathering screenshots, exporting configurations, and compiling spreadsheets. CompliLLM transforms compliance into a continuous, automated process. The platform integrates with over 100 enterprise tools (cloud providers, identity providers, HR systems, code repositories, ticketing platforms, endpoint managers) to pull real-time evidence that controls are operating effectively. When a control drifts out of compliance, CompliLLM alerts the responsible owner immediately and provides remediation guidance.

CompliLLM's AI engine goes beyond checkbox compliance. It analyzes the relationships between controls, identifies redundancies and conflicts, scores vendor risk, and generates natural language audit narratives that explain not just whether a control is met, but how the organization is meeting it and what evidence supports that conclusion.

## Key Features

- **Automated Evidence Collection** — Integrates with 100+ enterprise tools (AWS, Azure, GCP, Okta, GitHub, Jira, Slack, Workday, Gusto, Jamf, CrowdStrike, and more) to continuously collect compliance evidence without human intervention. Evidence is timestamped, hashed for integrity, and stored in a tamper-proof evidence vault.
- **Continuous Control Monitoring** — Real-time monitoring of organizational controls against framework requirements. Monitors configuration drift, access control changes, encryption status, backup completion, vulnerability scan results, and security awareness training completion. Alerts on control failures within minutes.
- **Multi-Framework Support** — Built-in support for SOC 2 Type I/II, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS v4.0, NIST 800-53 Rev 5, NIST CSF 2.0, FedRAMP Moderate/High, CMMC Level 2, CCPA/CPRA, and custom frameworks. Cross-mapping engine identifies shared controls across frameworks to reduce duplicate effort.
- **Audit-Ready Reports** — One-click generation of audit-ready report packages including control matrices, evidence bundles, gap analysis summaries, and remediation tracking. Reports are formatted for direct consumption by external auditors (Big 4, regional firms, and independent assessors).
- **Policy Management** — Centralized policy library with version control, approval workflows, employee acknowledgment tracking, and automatic review reminders. Pre-built policy templates aligned to major frameworks, customizable to each organization's needs.
- **Gap Analysis** — AI-powered gap analysis that compares current control posture against target framework requirements, prioritizes gaps by risk impact, and generates detailed remediation plans with estimated effort and recommended owners.
- **Vendor Risk Scoring** — Automated assessment of third-party vendor security posture based on SOC 2 reports, security questionnaire responses, publicly available breach data, and external attack surface scans. Continuous monitoring of vendor risk with alerts on material changes.
- **Natural Language Audit Narratives** — LLM-powered generation of control descriptions, test procedures, and audit conclusions in natural language. Saves compliance teams 20+ hours per audit cycle on documentation.
