## Background

Zara Obi is a Technical Writer at CyberLLM, responsible for creating and maintaining API
documentation, user guides, knowledge-base articles, and the public-facing developer docs portal
for all eight CyberLLM products. Before CyberLLM, she spent three years at Snyk as a Senior
Technical Writer, where she owned the documentation for Snyk's Container Security and
Infrastructure as Code products. At Snyk, she built a docs-as-code pipeline (Markdown + Git +
static site generator), wrote API reference documentation covering 200+ endpoints, and created
onboarding tutorials that reduced customer time-to-first-value by 35%. Her documentation was
consistently rated as a competitive advantage in customer surveys, and she established Snyk's
style guide and documentation review process.

Prior to Snyk, Zara worked as a technical writer at Cloudflare's London office for two years,
documenting the Workers (serverless) and Access (zero-trust) product lines. She earned her BA in
English Literature from the University of Edinburgh and a postgraduate diploma in Technical
Communication from the University of Limerick. Her career pivot from literature to technical
writing came after discovering she could combine her love of clear prose with her fascination for
technology during a coding bootcamp. She is proficient in Markdown, reStructuredText, OpenAPI
specification, and docs-as-code toolchains (Docusaurus, GitBook, and ReadMe).

Zara joined CyberLLM after being recruited by the product team, who identified documentation as a
critical gap following customer feedback in Q4 2022. At CyberLLM, she audited and rewrote the
existing ad-hoc documentation (scattered across Notion, Confluence, and inline code comments) into
a unified developer docs portal built on Docusaurus. She maintains API reference documentation for
all eight products (ThreatLLM, ShieldLLM, NetLLM, VaultLLM, CompliLLM, IncidentLLM, PenLLM, and
IdentLLM), writes user guides and admin manuals, creates integration tutorials, and manages the
customer-facing knowledge base. She works closely with engineering, product, and customer success
teams to ensure documentation stays current with each product release.
