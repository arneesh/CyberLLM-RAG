## Background

Kwame Asante is a Security Researcher at CyberLLM, specializing in vulnerability discovery,
exploit development, and offensive security research. He has discovered three zero-day
vulnerabilities published under CyberLLM's responsible disclosure program, earning coordinated
CVE assignments and recognition from affected vendors. His research directly feeds PenLLM's
vulnerability database and detection signatures, making him a critical contributor to
CyberLLM's offensive security product capabilities. Before joining CyberLLM, he spent four
years as a cyber analyst at GCHQ (Government Communications Headquarters), the UK's signals
intelligence and cybersecurity agency, where he worked in the National Cyber Security Centre
(NCSC) division on vulnerability assessment and threat analysis for UK critical infrastructure.

Prior to GCHQ, Kwame earned his MSc in Information Security from Royal Holloway, University of
London, where his thesis focused on side-channel attacks against hardware security modules. He
holds a BSc in Computer Science from the University of Ghana, Legon, and relocated to the UK
for graduate studies on a Chevening Scholarship. He maintains Offensive Security Certified
Professional (OSCP) and GIAC Exploit Researcher and Advanced Penetration Tester (GXPN)
certifications. His combination of government intelligence experience and deep technical
vulnerability research skills gives CyberLLM a rare offensive security perspective.

Kwame was recruited by Priya Raghavan through an intelligence community networking event in
London. At CyberLLM, he conducts independent vulnerability research against widely deployed
enterprise software, writes detection signatures for PenLLM's scanning engine, and contributes
attack simulation scenarios to ShieldLLM's behavioral detection test suite. He works remotely
from London — CyberLLM's first and only UK-based employee — and collaborates asynchronously
with the Austin team, overlapping 4-5 hours daily. He visits Austin twice annually for
research sprints and company all-hands events.
