## Background

Ben Harrington is a Sales Development Representative (SDR) at CyberLLM, responsible for
outbound prospecting, lead qualification, and booking discovery meetings for the account executive
team. CyberLLM is his first professional sales role. He graduated from Fordham University in May
2023 with a BA in Business Administration and a minor in Computer Science. During college, he
interned at a B2B SaaS startup in their marketing department, where he gained exposure to lead
generation, CRM management, and outbound email campaigns. He was a captain of Fordham's club
lacrosse team and credits his competitive sports background with giving him the resilience and
work ethic needed for outbound sales.

Ben grew up on Long Island, New York, and became interested in cybersecurity sales after attending
a panel on AI-driven security at Fordham's entrepreneurship series, where a CyberLLM customer
spoke about implementing ThreatLLM. He applied through CyberLLM's careers page and was hired by
Sarah Mitchell, who saw his energy and coachability as strong fits for a junior SDR role.

At CyberLLM, Ben is responsible for generating qualified meetings through cold outreach — email
sequences, LinkedIn engagement, and cold calls — targeting CISOs, VP Security, and IT directors
at mid-market and enterprise companies. He uses Outreach.io for sequencing, LinkedIn Sales
Navigator for prospecting, and Salesforce for pipeline management. His first six months were
marked by inconsistency: he exceeded quota in his first and third months but fell short in months
two, four, and five, struggling with objection handling and prioritizing high-value accounts over
high-volume activity. Sarah Mitchell paired him with a structured mentorship program including
weekly call reviews, role-playing sessions, and shadowing senior AEs on discovery calls. His
performance stabilized in Q1 2024.
