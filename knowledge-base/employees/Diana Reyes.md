## Background

Diana Reyes is the Customer Success Manager at CyberLLM, responsible for onboarding, adoption,
retention, and expansion of enterprise accounts. She manages a portfolio of 35 enterprise
customers with a combined ARR of $8.5M and maintains a 95% renewal rate — the highest in
CyberLLM's history. Before joining CyberLLM, she spent three years as a Customer Success
Manager at Tenable, where she managed a portfolio of 50+ enterprise accounts using Tenable's
vulnerability management platform. At Tenable, she developed a health scoring framework that
predicted churn risk with 85% accuracy and drove a 12% increase in net revenue retention
through proactive expansion plays.

Prior to Tenable, Diana worked for two years in technical account management at a New York-based
identity security startup, where she supported customer onboarding and adoption for a
zero-trust access management product. She holds a BA in Business Administration from Fordham
University and an MS in Information Systems from NYU, where she focused on customer analytics
and technology adoption models. She is a Certified Customer Success Manager (CCSM) through
SuccessHACKER and a Gainsight Administrator.

Diana was recruited by Sarah Mitchell, who recognized the need for a dedicated customer success
function as CyberLLM's enterprise customer base grew beyond 20 accounts. Diana was the
company's first CSM hire and has built the customer success practice from scratch. She
implemented Gainsight as CyberLLM's customer success platform, designed health scoring models,
and created a structured 90-day onboarding program that has reduced time-to-value from 60 days
to 21 days. She partners closely with Olivia Foster on account transitions from sales to
success and with James Thornton on technical onboarding support.
