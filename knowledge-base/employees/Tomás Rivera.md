## Background

Tomás Rivera is the Product Manager responsible for two of CyberLLM's product lines: ShieldLLM
(endpoint detection and response) and IncidentLLM (security orchestration, automation, and
response). Managing two products simultaneously requires exceptional prioritization skills, and
Tomás has built a reputation for balancing competing roadmaps without sacrificing delivery
quality. Before joining CyberLLM, he spent four years at Carbon Black (acquired by VMware),
where he rose from Associate PM to Product Lead for the endpoint detection module. His team
shipped the behavioral detection engine that became Carbon Black's highest-rated feature in
Forrester Wave evaluations.

Prior to Carbon Black, Tomás worked as a security operations center (SOC) analyst at a large
financial services firm for two years, giving him firsthand experience with the incident
response workflows that IncidentLLM aims to automate. He holds a BS in Cybersecurity from NYU
Tandon School of Engineering and is a Certified Information Systems Security Professional
(CISSP). His practitioner background in SOC operations differentiates his product decisions —
he builds for analysts, not just for buyers.

Tomás was recruited by Sarah Mitchell, who had encountered him during a competitive deal where
Carbon Black and CyberLLM were both shortlisted. Impressed by his product knowledge during a
technical deep-dive, she introduced him to Marcus Chen. At CyberLLM, Tomás owns the ShieldLLM
and IncidentLLM roadmaps, coordinates closely with Raj Patel on behavioral ML models for
ShieldLLM, and works with Alex Petrov on automated triage playbook integration between
ThreatLLM and IncidentLLM. He operates from CyberLLM's New York office.
