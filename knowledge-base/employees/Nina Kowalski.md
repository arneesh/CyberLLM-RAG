## Background

Nina Kowalski is the Product Manager for ThreatLLM, CyberLLM's flagship threat intelligence
platform. She owns the ThreatLLM product roadmap, prioritizes feature development, and works
closely with Priya Raghavan's threat research team and Alex Petrov to translate intelligence
requirements into product capabilities. Before joining CyberLLM, she spent four years as a
Senior Product Manager at Recorded Future, where she managed the threat intelligence feed
product serving 600+ enterprise customers. At Recorded Future, she led the launch of an
automated IOC enrichment feature that became one of the platform's top-cited differentiators
in Gartner peer reviews.

Prior to Recorded Future, Nina worked for three years as a business analyst at Booz Allen
Hamilton supporting Department of Defense cybersecurity programs, where she gained deep
familiarity with government threat intelligence workflows and STIX/TAXII standards. She holds
a BA in International Relations from the University of Chicago and an MBA from MIT Sloan, where
she focused on technology product strategy. Her unique blend of national security domain
expertise and product management rigor makes her effective at bridging technical threat research
and customer-facing feature delivery.

Nina was recruited after a conversation with Marcus Chen at RSA Conference 2019, where she
presented on operationalizing threat intelligence at scale. She joined during a critical growth
period for ThreatLLM and has since shaped it from an internal research tool into a
market-competitive product with 40+ enterprise customers. She runs quarterly customer advisory
boards and maintains a Net Promoter Score tracking system that has driven several high-impact
feature decisions.
