## Background

Olivia Foster is an Account Executive at CyberLLM, managing mid-market and enterprise accounts
in the Northeast region. She reports to Sarah Mitchell and is one of CyberLLM's top-performing
sales representatives, consistently exceeding her annual quota. Before joining CyberLLM, she
spent three years as an Account Executive at Zscaler, where she sold cloud security solutions
to mid-market companies across financial services and manufacturing. In her final year at
Zscaler, she closed $4.2M in new annual recurring revenue, finishing at 138% of quota and
earning President's Club recognition.

Prior to Zscaler, Olivia worked for two years as a business development representative at
Palo Alto Networks, where she learned enterprise security sales fundamentals and developed her
prospecting methodology. She holds a BA in Communications from Boston University and completed
the Sandler Sales Training program and Force Management's Command of the Message certification.
Her consultative sales approach — focused on mapping CyberLLM's capabilities to specific
customer security gaps — has consistently resonated with technical buyers and CISOs.

Olivia was recruited by Sarah Mitchell, who had competed against her in several Zscaler deals
and recognized her talent for navigating complex enterprise procurement cycles. At CyberLLM,
Olivia manages a portfolio of 45 accounts across financial services, healthcare, and technology
verticals in the Northeast. She works closely with James Thornton on technical demos and
proof-of-concept engagements, and with Diana Reyes on post-sale customer transition and
expansion opportunities. She operates from CyberLLM's New York office.
