## Background

Hannah Bergström joined CyberLLM as a Compliance Analyst in April 2022, bringing deep expertise
in information security frameworks, regulatory audits, and risk assessments. Before CyberLLM, she
spent six years at KPMG's London office in the Technology Risk & Cybersecurity Advisory practice,
where she led ISO 27001 certification engagements and SOC 2 Type II audits for technology clients
ranging from Series B startups to FTSE 250 enterprises. She managed a portfolio of 12–15
concurrent audit engagements per year and was promoted to Senior Associate in 2020. Her clients
included major UK fintechs and SaaS providers, and she developed particular expertise in mapping
controls across overlapping frameworks — ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and PCI DSS.

Hannah holds a BSc in Information Security from Royal Holloway, University of London, and is a
Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) and ISO 27001 Lead Auditor. She grew up in
Gothenburg, Sweden, and moved to London for university, staying on after graduation. Her bilingual
Swedish-English background and European regulatory knowledge have been valuable for CyberLLM's
expansion into UK and EU markets, where compliance requirements differ significantly from U.S.
frameworks.

Hannah was recruited after meeting David Okonkwo at the InfoSecurity Europe conference in 2021,
where she attended a CyberLLM demo of CompliLLM. Impressed by the product's AI-driven approach to
compliance automation, she reached out about contributing domain expertise. At CyberLLM, Hannah
serves as the primary subject matter expert for CompliLLM's framework templates and audit
workflows. She authored the ISO 27001 and SOC 2 control mapping libraries that CompliLLM uses to
auto-generate audit evidence packages, reducing customer audit prep time by an estimated 60%. She
also manages CyberLLM's own internal compliance posture, having led the company through its first
SOC 2 Type II certification in 2023.
