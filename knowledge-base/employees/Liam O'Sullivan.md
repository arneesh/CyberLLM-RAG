## Background

Liam O'Sullivan is the DevSecOps Engineer at CyberLLM, responsible for the CI/CD pipelines,
infrastructure-as-code, container security, and deployment automation that underpin all eight
CyberLLM products. Before joining CyberLLM, he spent three years as a DevOps consultant at
HashiCorp, where he helped Fortune 500 enterprises adopt Terraform, Vault, and Consul for
cloud infrastructure management. His largest engagement was a 14-month project at a major U.S.
insurance company, migrating 400+ services from on-premises data centers to AWS using Terraform
and implementing secrets management with HashiCorp Vault — an experience that gave him deep
familiarity with regulated industries' security and compliance requirements.

Prior to HashiCorp, Liam worked as a systems administrator at a Portland-based SaaS startup for
two years, where he first built CI/CD pipelines using Jenkins and GitLab CI and developed his
passion for automating security into the software delivery lifecycle. He holds a BS in
Information Systems from Oregon State University and maintains HashiCorp Certified Terraform
Associate and AWS Solutions Architect Professional certifications.

Liam was hired by David Okonkwo to formalize CyberLLM's DevSecOps practices as the company
scaled from five products to eight. He rebuilt the entire CI/CD infrastructure on GitHub Actions
with custom security scanning gates — integrating SAST, DAST, SCA, and container image scanning
into every pull request. He manages the Kubernetes clusters running CyberLLM's SaaS platform
and has automated infrastructure provisioning so that spinning up a new customer tenant takes
under four minutes. He works remotely from Portland and visits Austin quarterly.
