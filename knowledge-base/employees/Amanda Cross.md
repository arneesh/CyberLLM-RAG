## Background

Amanda Cross is the HR Business Partner at CyberLLM, serving as the sole People Operations
professional for the company's 35-person team. Before CyberLLM, she spent eight years as the
HR Director at TechStream Solutions, a 200-person mid-market SaaS company in Austin that was
acquired by a private equity firm in 2020. At TechStream, she built the HR function from a
one-person operation to a three-person team, implementing performance management systems,
compensation frameworks, benefits administration, DEI programs, and employee engagement
initiatives. She led the company through three rounds of hypergrowth hiring (from 60 to 200
employees) and managed the HR integration during the PE acquisition, ensuring minimal employee
disruption and 95% retention of key talent.

Amanda earned her BA in Psychology from Texas A&M University and her MA in Industrial-
Organizational Psychology from the University of Houston. She holds SHRM-SCP and PHR
certifications and is a member of Austin's HR Tech Council. Prior to TechStream, she worked
as an HR generalist at Dell Technologies for three years, gaining experience in enterprise
HR processes, compensation benchmarking, and organizational design.

After TechStream's acquisition, Amanda was looking for an opportunity to build HR at another
high-growth startup. Marcus Chen recruited her after a referral from a mutual Austin tech
community connection. At CyberLLM, Amanda manages the full spectrum of People Operations:
recruiting, onboarding, performance management, compensation and benefits, employee relations,
compliance, learning and development, and DEI initiatives. She designed CyberLLM's performance
review process (quarterly check-ins with annual comprehensive reviews), compensation philosophy
(market-rate base + equity + performance bonuses), and the structured rotation program for junior
hires that Tyler Washington currently participates in. She is the confidential point of contact
for all employee concerns and manages relationships with CyberLLM's benefits providers, PEO, and
external employment counsel.
