## What We Do

CyberLLM develops AI-driven cybersecurity products that help enterprises detect, prevent, and respond to cyber threats. Our platform suite spans the full security lifecycle — from threat intelligence and endpoint protection to compliance automation and identity governance. Each product leverages large language models, deep learning, and real-time data processing to deliver intelligent, adaptive security that scales with the complexity of modern enterprise environments.

Our products process over 4 billion security events per day across all deployed clients, providing real-time threat detection, automated incident response, and continuous compliance monitoring.

## Product Portfolio

CyberLLM offers eight distinct products, each addressing a critical domain of enterprise cybersecurity:

### 1. ThreatLLM — Threat Intelligence & Detection
AI-powered threat intelligence platform that aggregates global threat feeds, monitors the dark web, correlates indicators of compromise (IOCs), and profiles threat actors in real time. Used by security operations centers (SOCs) to stay ahead of emerging threats.

**Active Clients**: 5
**Key Verticals**: Financial Services, Government, Defense

### 2. ShieldLLM — Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)
Next-generation EDR solution with AI behavioral analysis, real-time endpoint monitoring, automated threat containment, and forensic timeline reconstruction. Detects zero-day exploits and fileless attacks that signature-based tools miss.

**Active Clients**: 4
**Key Verticals**: Healthcare, Retail, Technology

### 3. NetLLM — Network Security & SIEM
AI-driven network security monitoring and SIEM platform. Performs deep packet inspection, anomaly detection, log aggregation, and correlation analysis. Customizable dashboards and automated playbook triggers enable rapid response to network-based threats.

**Active Clients**: 4
**Key Verticals**: Financial Services, Energy, Government

### 4. VaultLLM — Data Protection & Encryption
Comprehensive data protection platform with AI-powered data classification, data loss prevention (DLP) policies, end-to-end encryption, key management, and cloud storage scanning. Ensures sensitive data remains secure across hybrid and multi-cloud environments.

**Active Clients**: 4
**Key Verticals**: Healthcare, Financial Services, Government

### 5. CompliLLM — Compliance Automation
Automated compliance platform that continuously monitors organizational controls against SOC 2, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, and NIST frameworks. Generates audit-ready reports, manages policies, identifies control gaps, and scores vendor risk.

**Active Clients**: 5
**Key Verticals**: Financial Services, Healthcare, Retail

### 6. IncidentLLM — Incident Response & SOAR
Security orchestration, automation, and response (SOAR) platform. Automates incident triage, orchestrates cross-tool playbooks, reconstructs attack timelines, generates post-incident reports, and provides a war room for real-time collaboration during active incidents.

**Active Clients**: 5
**Key Verticals**: Government, Defense, Energy

### 7. PenLLM — Vulnerability Management & Pen Testing
Continuous vulnerability scanning and AI-assisted penetration testing platform. Prioritizes vulnerabilities by exploitability and business impact, maps attack paths, provides remediation guidance, and integrates with CI/CD pipelines for shift-left security.

**Active Clients**: 4
**Key Verticals**: Technology, Retail, Financial Services

### 8. IdentLLM — Identity & Access Management / Zero Trust
AI-powered identity and access management platform with Zero Trust policy enforcement. Provides SSO/MFA integration, privileged access management (PAM), behavioral authentication, identity governance, and adaptive access decisions based on real-time risk scoring.

**Active Clients**: 4
**Key Verticals**: Financial Services, Government, Healthcare

## Verticals Served

CyberLLM serves enterprise clients across seven major industry verticals:

- **Financial Services** — Banks, insurance companies, fintech firms, and investment management firms. Our largest vertical, with clients relying on ThreatLLM, NetLLM, VaultLLM, CompliLLM, PenLLM, and IdentLLM.
- **Healthcare** — Hospital systems, health insurers, and pharmaceutical companies. Clients leverage ShieldLLM for endpoint protection, VaultLLM for PHI data protection, CompliLLM for HIPAA compliance, and IdentLLM for provider identity governance.
- **Government** — Federal civilian agencies, state governments, and municipal entities. Clients use ThreatLLM, NetLLM, VaultLLM, IncidentLLM, and IdentLLM. CyberLLM holds FedRAMP Moderate authorization.
- **Retail** — E-commerce platforms, brick-and-mortar chains, and consumer brands. Clients rely on ShieldLLM for POS endpoint security, CompliLLM for PCI-DSS compliance, and PenLLM for web application testing.
- **Energy** — Utilities, oil and gas, and renewable energy companies. Clients use NetLLM for OT/IT network monitoring and IncidentLLM for ICS incident response.
- **Technology** — SaaS companies, cloud providers, and semiconductor firms. Clients leverage ShieldLLM, PenLLM, and our CI/CD security integrations.
- **Defense** — Defense contractors and military-adjacent organizations. Clients use ThreatLLM for adversary tracking and IncidentLLM for classified environment incident response.

## Competitive Positioning

CyberLLM differentiates from traditional cybersecurity vendors (CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, Splunk, SentinelOne) through:

1. **AI-native architecture** — Products are built on LLM and deep learning foundations, not retrofitted with AI layers.
2. **Unified platform** — Eight products share a common data lake, threat intelligence backbone, and management console, enabling correlated insights across the full security stack.
3. **Speed to value** — Average deployment time of 14 days from contract signature to production, compared to 60-90 days for legacy vendors.
4. **Lean pricing** — Enterprise-grade security at mid-market price points, enabled by AI automation that reduces the need for large SOC teams.
