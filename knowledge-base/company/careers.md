# Careers at CyberLLM

## Why CyberLLM?

Cyber threats are evolving faster than human teams can keep up. At CyberLLM, we're building the AI-powered security platform that tips the scales back in the defenders' favor. Joining our team means working on problems that matter — protecting hospitals from ransomware, shielding financial systems from fraud, and helping governments defend critical infrastructure — using cutting-edge AI and machine learning techniques.

We are a 35-person company with the impact of a team three times our size. Every person here is a force multiplier. There are no passengers, no bureaucracy, and no busywork. You'll ship code that protects real organizations from real threats, and you'll see the impact of your work within days, not quarters.

## What It's Like to Work Here

- **High autonomy, high impact** — You own your work end to end. No micromanagement, no endless approval chains.
- **World-class colleagues** — Our team includes former NSA analysts, Google Staff Engineers, CrowdStrike product leaders, and published ML researchers.
- **Continuous learning** — $3,000 annual conference and learning budget, monthly CTF challenges, Hack Weeks, and Lunch & Learns.
- **Remote-friendly** — Work from any of our four offices (Austin, NYC, SF, London) or fully remote within the US, UK, or EU.
- **Security-first culture** — Everyone here thinks like a defender. Security isn't just the product we sell — it's how we operate.

## Our Hiring Process

1. **Application Review** — Our recruiting team reviews every application within 5 business days.
2. **Recruiter Screen** — 30-minute call to discuss the role, your background, and mutual fit.
3. **Hiring Manager Interview** — 45-minute deep dive into your experience and the role's challenges.
4. **Technical Assessment** — Role-specific exercise completed asynchronously (engineering: take-home coding challenge; security research: threat analysis report; product: product spec; sales: mock pitch).
5. **Team Interviews** — 2-3 interviews with cross-functional team members focused on collaboration, problem-solving, and culture fit.
6. **Offer** — Competitive offer extended within 48 hours of final interview.

We are committed to a fair and inclusive hiring process. All interviews use structured formats with standardized rubrics. We provide accommodations for candidates who need them.
