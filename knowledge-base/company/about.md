# About CyberLLM

## Who We Are

CyberLLM is an AI-driven cybersecurity company headquartered in Austin, Texas. Founded in 2018 by **Marcus Chen** (CEO) and **Dr. Elena Vasquez** (CTO), CyberLLM builds intelligent security products that help enterprises detect, prevent, and respond to cyber threats at machine speed. Our platform combines large language models, deep learning, and decades of security domain expertise to deliver protection that adapts as fast as the threat landscape evolves.

## Our Origin Story

CyberLLM was born in the cybersecurity research lab at the University of Texas at Austin. Marcus Chen, a serial entrepreneur with two previous exits in the security space, and Dr. Elena Vasquez, a leading researcher in adversarial machine learning and network intrusion detection, recognized that traditional rule-based security tools were falling behind increasingly sophisticated threat actors. In late 2017, they began prototyping an AI engine capable of ingesting massive volumes of threat intelligence, correlating indicators of compromise across disparate feeds, and surfacing actionable insights in natural language.

By early 2018, they incorporated CyberLLM, Inc. and secured a $2.5M seed round led by Sentinel Ventures with participation from Austin-based angel investors. The founding team of five — Marcus, Elena, two ML engineers, and a security researcher — set up shop in a co-working space on East 6th Street and shipped their first product, ThreatLLM, to a handful of beta customers within nine months.

## Growth and Funding

- **2018**: Company founded. Seed round of $2.5M. First product (ThreatLLM) enters private beta with three financial services firms.
- **2019**: Series A of $12M led by CrestPoint Capital. Team grows to 18 employees. ShieldLLM and NetLLM launch. First government contract signed with a federal civilian agency.
- **2020**: Despite the pandemic, CyberLLM experiences 140% YoY revenue growth as remote work drives demand for endpoint and network security. Team reaches 30 employees. VaultLLM and CompliLLM launch. Austin HQ moves to a dedicated office in the Domain district.
- **2021**: Series B of $45M led by Apex Growth Partners with participation from CrestPoint Capital and strategic angel investors from the cybersecurity industry. Team peaks at 50 employees. NYC and SF satellite offices open. IncidentLLM and PenLLM launch. ARR crosses $20M.
- **2022**: IdentLLM launches, completing the eight-product portfolio. London office opens to serve EMEA clients. ARR reaches $25M. The company invests heavily in R&D for next-generation LLM-powered security automation.
- **2023**: Following a broader market correction in the tech sector, CyberLLM restructures to a lean, high-performance team of 35 employees. The company focuses on profitability, operational efficiency, and deepening relationships with existing enterprise clients. ARR grows to $28M despite the smaller headcount.
- **2024**: CyberLLM achieves cash-flow positive operations. The company renews 100% of enterprise contracts and expands into the energy and defense verticals. The product suite processes over 4 billion security events per day across all clients.

## Certifications and Compliance

CyberLLM maintains SOC 2 Type II certification, ISO 27001 compliance, and FedRAMP Authorization at the Moderate impact level. The company undergoes annual third-party penetration testing and publishes transparency reports on its security posture. All products are developed in accordance with NIST Cybersecurity Framework guidelines and support CISA's Secure by Design principles.
