# SaaS Subscription Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Silverline Insurance Co. — CompliLLM Platform

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2025
**Contract Number:** CL-SIC-2025-0134

---

## 1. Parties

- **Provider:** CyberLLM Inc., a Delaware corporation, with principal offices at 440 Security Boulevard, Suite 1200, Austin, TX 78701 ("CyberLLM").
- **Client:** Silverline Insurance Co., an Oregon domestic insurance company, with principal offices at 900 SW Fifth Avenue, Suite 400, Portland, OR 97204 ("Client").

## 2. Term and Effective Date

This Agreement shall commence on September 1, 2025 and continue for an initial term of twelve (12) months, expiring on August 31, 2026 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8.

## 3. Subscription Tier and Monthly Fee

- **Tier:** CompliLLM Starter
- **Monthly Subscription Fee:** $3,500.00 USD
- **One-Time Setup Fee:** $5,000.00 USD (due upon execution)

## 4. Scope of Services and Features

CyberLLM shall provide Client access to the CompliLLM Starter platform, which includes:

- SOC 2 Type I audit preparation toolkit including pre-built control templates, evidence collection checklists, and readiness assessment scoring.
- Gap analysis engine that evaluates Client's current security posture against SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria and generates a prioritized remediation roadmap.
- Basic policy management module with a library of customizable policy templates (information security, acceptable use, incident response, vendor management, and data classification).
- Policy attestation tracking for up to fifty (50) employees with automated reminder workflows.
- Compliance posture dashboard with high-level KPIs and trend reporting.
- Document repository for storing and organizing audit evidence, policies, and risk assessments.
- Quarterly automated risk assessment questionnaire aligned to NIST CSF Identify and Protect functions.
- Email-based compliance alerts for control failures and upcoming policy review deadlines.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** CyberLLM guarantees 99.5% monthly uptime for the CompliLLM Starter platform.
- **Scheduled Maintenance:** Maintenance shall be performed during weekend windows (Saturday 12:00 AM–6:00 AM CT) with at least 48 hours' notice.
- **Incident Response:** Critical issues shall receive initial response within four (4) business hours.
- **SLA Credits:** If monthly uptime falls below 99.5%, Client shall receive a 5% credit on that month's subscription fee for each full percentage point below the threshold, capped at 15%.

## 6. Payment Terms

- Subscription fees are billed monthly on the first business day of each month.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date via ACH, check, or corporate credit card.
- Late payments shall incur a finance charge of 1.0% per month on the outstanding balance.
- The one-time setup fee is invoiced at execution and is non-refundable.

## 7. Support

- **Support Tier:** Standard Support (Starter tier)
- **Channels:** Email and in-platform ticketing system.
- **Response Times:** Critical issues within 4 business hours; standard issues within 2 business days.
- **Training:** One (1) remote onboarding session (up to 2 hours) covering platform setup, policy configuration, and gap analysis workflow.
- **Knowledge Base:** Unlimited access to CyberLLM's self-service documentation portal and compliance resource library.

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within fifteen (15) days.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon thirty (30) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to two (2) months of the subscription fee.
- Upon termination, Client's access to the platform shall cease at the end of the notice period. CyberLLM shall make Client data available for export for thirty (30) days following termination.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing may increase by up to 7% with at least forty-five (45) days' advance written notice.

## 10. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all non-public information disclosed by the other party. CyberLLM shall store all Client data in U.S.-based data centers with encryption at rest and in transit. CyberLLM acknowledges that Client is subject to state insurance regulatory oversight and shall cooperate with any regulatory examination requests related to the services provided hereunder.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Neither party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall exceed the total fees paid or payable by Client during the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages.
