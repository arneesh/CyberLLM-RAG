# Master Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Emerald Gaming Corp — PenLLM Essentials

**Agreement Number:** CL-PLM-2024-0312
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2024
**Document Classification:** Confidential

---

## 1. Parties

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Emerald Gaming Corp**, a Nevada corporation with principal offices at 4500 Neon Strip Avenue, Las Vegas, NV 89109 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Duration

This Agreement shall commence on June 1, 2024, and shall continue for a period of **twelve (12) months**, terminating on May 31, 2025, unless renewed or terminated in accordance with the provisions herein.

## 3. Product and Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **PenLLM Essentials** — an AI-driven vulnerability management and penetration testing platform — configured for online gaming and entertainment environments. The scope includes:

- Automated web application vulnerability scanning for Client's online gaming portal, player account management system, and promotional microsites
- Monthly DDoS resilience assessments simulating volumetric, protocol, and application-layer attack scenarios against Client's game servers and matchmaking infrastructure
- Basic AI-assisted penetration testing targeting Client's external-facing web applications and APIs, with OWASP Top 10 coverage
- Vulnerability dashboard with severity-based prioritization and trend tracking over the contract period
- Integration with Client's PagerDuty alerting system for critical and high-severity findings
- Semi-annual executive summary reports suitable for presentation to Client's Board of Directors

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Platform Fee | $3,000.00 |
| One-Time Setup & Onboarding Fee | $5,000.00 |
| DDoS Resilience Assessments (Monthly) | Included |

- All fees are billed monthly in arrears. Invoices are issued on the 1st of each calendar month.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less.
- The one-time setup fee shall be invoiced upon execution of this Agreement and is due within thirty (30) days.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Provider guarantees the following service levels:

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.5% measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows
- **Scan Completion:** Full web application scan completed within 6 hours of initiation
- **DDoS Assessment Report Delivery:** Within 5 business days of assessment completion
- **Critical Vulnerability Notification:** Within 4 hours of validated discovery
- **SLA Credits:** For each 0.5% below the uptime guarantee, Client shall receive a credit of 5% of that month's fee, up to a maximum of 15%

## 6. Support

Client shall receive **Tier 3 — Standard Support**, which includes:

- Business hours support (Monday–Friday, 9 AM–6 PM CT) via email and support portal
- Target first response within 8 business hours for standard issues, 4 business hours for critical severity findings
- Access to CyberLLM's self-service knowledge base, community forums, and remediation guides
- Quarterly account review call with a CyberLLM Customer Success representative
- Escalation to senior engineers available for critical platform outages only

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information exchanged under this Agreement. Confidential information includes, but is not limited to, vulnerability scan results, penetration test findings, application architecture details, player data handling practices, and financial terms. This obligation survives termination for a period of three (3) years.

## 8. Data Handling and Compliance

- All scan data and findings shall be encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Provider shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification
- Provider acknowledges Client's obligations under Nevada Gaming Control Board regulations and PCI-DSS requirements for payment processing systems, and shall format reports to support Client's compliance evidence needs

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least thirty (30) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing shall not increase by more than 5% annually without mutual written agreement.

## 10. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon thirty (30) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision and fails to cure such breach within the notice period.
- Client may terminate for convenience with sixty (60) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to 30% of the remaining monthly fees for the unexpired term.
- Upon termination, Provider shall securely destroy or return all Client data within thirty (30) days and provide written certification of destruction.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.
