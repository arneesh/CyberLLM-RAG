# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Quantum Retail Corp — ThreatLLM Essentials

**Agreement Number:** CL-TLM-2024-0284
**Effective Date:** June 15, 2024

---

## 1. Parties

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Quantum Retail Corp**, 900 Commerce Parkway, Minneapolis, MN 55402 ("Client")

## 2. Term

Twelve (12) months, from June 15, 2024, through June 14, 2025.

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ThreatLLM Essentials**, an AI-powered threat intelligence platform. Services include:

- Curated threat intelligence feeds relevant to the retail and e-commerce sector
- PCI-DSS focused threat monitoring: detection of payment card skimming campaigns, point-of-sale malware, and Magecart-style attacks
- E-commerce fraud intelligence including credential stuffing campaign tracking and account takeover indicators
- Automated alerting via email and webhook integration with Client's existing monitoring tools
- Monthly threat summary reports with retail sector context
- Access to CyberLLM's Threat Intelligence Portal with search and IOC lookup capabilities

## 4. Fees

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription | $3,000.00 |
| Setup Fee | $5,000.00 |

- Monthly invoices issued on the 1st; payment due net 30.
- Late payments subject to 1.5% monthly interest.
- Setup fee invoiced upon execution.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.5% monthly
- **Alert Delivery:** Within 60 minutes of threat validation
- **SLA Credit:** 10% of monthly fee if uptime falls below 99.5%, maximum 20% credit per month

## 6. Support

Client receives **Tier 3 — Standard Support**:

- Business hours support: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM CT, Monday–Friday
- Critical issues: 4-hour response time
- Non-critical issues: next business day response
- Email and ticketing portal access
- Quarterly business review calls

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to protect confidential information exchanged under this Agreement. This obligation survives for two (2) years following termination.

## 8. Data Handling

- All data encrypted at rest and in transit
- Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II certification
- Client data stored in U.S.-based data centers
- Provider shall not share Client-specific threat data with third parties

## 9. Renewal

Auto-renews for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides thirty (30) days' written notice before the end of the current term.

## 10. Termination

- **For Cause:** Either party may terminate with thirty (30) days' notice for uncured material breach.
- **For Convenience:** Client may terminate with sixty (60) days' notice, subject to an early termination fee of two (2) months' subscription fees.
- Provider will delete Client data within thirty (30) days of termination.
