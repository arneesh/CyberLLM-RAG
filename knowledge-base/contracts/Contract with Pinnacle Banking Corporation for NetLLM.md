# Master Services Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Pinnacle Banking Corporation — NetLLM Enterprise Platform

**Document ID:** CL-NLM-2024-0917  
**Effective Date:** September 1, 2024  
**Prepared By:** CyberLLM Legal & Enterprise Sales

---

## 1. Parties

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cyber Ridge Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Pinnacle Banking Corporation**, a federally chartered financial institution headquartered at 200 Wall Street Tower, New York, NY 10005 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Effective Date

- **Effective Date:** September 1, 2024
- **Initial Term:** Thirty-six (36) months, concluding August 31, 2027
- **Contract Value (Initial Term):** $792,000

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the **NetLLM Enterprise Platform** configured for multi-site financial institution deployment, including:

- Real-time network security monitoring and AI-powered SIEM across all **twelve (12) branch locations** and the Client's primary data center
- Integration with Client's core banking transaction monitoring systems for real-time anomaly detection on wire transfers, ACH, and SWIFT messaging
- SOX (Sarbanes-Oxley Act) compliance dashboards with automated audit trail generation and quarterly compliance reporting
- Dedicated CyberLLM SIEM Analyst embedded with Client's Security Operations Center (SOC) during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM ET)
- Custom threat intelligence feeds tailored to the financial services sector, including FS-ISAC integration
- Advanced persistent threat (APT) detection models trained on banking-specific attack vectors
- Encrypted log aggregation with 7-year retention to meet federal banking regulatory requirements
- Executive-level monthly threat briefings and quarterly board-ready risk reports

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Platform Fee | $22,000 |
| One-Time Setup & Deployment Fee | $45,000 |
| Dedicated Analyst Supplement (included) | Included in monthly fee |

- All invoices are issued on the first business day of each calendar month and are payable within **thirty (30) days** of the invoice date.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of **1.5% per month** or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.
- The one-time setup fee shall be invoiced upon execution of this Agreement and is due prior to deployment commencement.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.95% measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows
- **Critical Incident Response:** Within fifteen (15) minutes of detection, 24/7/365
- **High-Severity Incident Response:** Within one (1) hour
- **Medium/Low-Severity Response:** Within four (4) business hours
- **SLA Credit:** Client shall receive a 5% credit on the monthly fee for each 0.1% below the 99.95% uptime threshold, capped at 30% of the monthly fee.

## 6. Support

- **Tier:** Platinum Enterprise Support
- **Dedicated Account Team:** Named Technical Account Manager, Solutions Architect, and embedded SIEM Analyst
- **Support Channels:** Direct phone line, encrypted email, secure Slack channel, on-site as needed
- **Escalation Path:** Severity-1 incidents escalate to CyberLLM VP of Engineering within thirty (30) minutes

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties acknowledge that the performance of this Agreement may involve the exchange of Confidential Information, including but not limited to Client's customer financial data, network topology, and security posture assessments. All Confidential Information shall be protected in accordance with the mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement executed between the parties on July 15, 2024, which is incorporated herein by reference. Provider agrees to comply with the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) and all applicable federal and state data protection regulations.

## 8. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive **twelve (12) month** periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **ninety (90) days** prior to the expiration of the then-current term. Renewal pricing shall be subject to an annual escalation not exceeding **4%** of the prior term's monthly fee.

## 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon **sixty (60) days** written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision and fails to cure within that period.
- Client may terminate for convenience with **one hundred twenty (120) days** written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to **six (6) months** of the monthly platform fee.
- Provider may terminate immediately if Client fails to pay any undisputed invoice within sixty (60) days of its due date.
- Upon termination, Provider shall cooperate in an orderly transition of services and data over a period not to exceed ninety (90) days.
