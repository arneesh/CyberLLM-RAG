# Subscription Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Heritage Financial Services — VaultLLM Basic

**Document ID:** CL-VLM-2025-0145  
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025  
**Prepared By:** CyberLLM SMB Sales

---

## 1. Parties

This Subscription Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cyber Ridge Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Heritage Financial Services**, a registered investment advisory firm organized under the laws of Connecticut, with offices at 75 Elm Street, Suite 310, Hartford, CT 06103 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Effective Date

- **Effective Date:** February 1, 2025
- **Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, concluding January 31, 2026
- **Contract Value (Initial Term):** $24,000

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the **VaultLLM Basic** platform configured for small-scale wealth management operations, including:

- AES-256 encryption for client personally identifiable information (PII), financial records, and portfolio documents at rest and in transit
- SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission) and FINRA (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority) compliance baseline reporting covering Regulation S-P and Regulation S-ID requirements
- Encrypted file sharing portal allowing secure document exchange between Client's advisors and their high-net-worth clientele
- Automated PII discovery and classification scanning across Client's document management system and email archives (up to 500 GB)
- Pre-configured data loss prevention (DLP) rules for financial document types including account statements, trade confirmations, and tax documents
- Monthly compliance summary reports accessible through Provider's web-based dashboard
- Encryption key management for up to fifty (50) active encryption keys with annual key rotation

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription Fee | $2,000 |
| One-Time Setup Fee | $3,500 |

- Invoices are issued on the first of each month and are due within **thirty (30) days**.
- Late payments shall incur a finance charge of **1.0% per month** on the outstanding balance.
- The setup fee covers initial deployment, PII discovery scan, DLP rule configuration, and a half-day virtual training session for Client's staff (up to 10 attendees).

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.5% measured on a monthly basis
- **Critical Incident Response:** Within one (1) hour during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM ET)
- **Standard Incident Response:** Within one (1) business day
- **After-Hours Support:** Best-effort basis for critical issues; emergency contact available with typical response within four (4) hours
- **SLA Credit:** If monthly uptime falls below 99.5%, Client shall receive a credit equal to 10% of that month's subscription fee.

## 6. Support

- **Tier:** Basic Support
- **Support Channels:** Email ticketing system with web-based portal access; phone support available during business hours
- **No Dedicated Account Manager:** Support is provided through Provider's general support queue with target first-response times as outlined in Section 5
- **Knowledge Base:** Full access to Provider's online documentation, compliance guides, and self-service configuration tutorials
- **Training:** Initial setup training included; additional training available at $1,200 per half-day session

## 7. Confidentiality

Provider acknowledges that Client's data includes highly sensitive financial information, client PII, and investment strategies that are subject to federal and state privacy regulations. Provider agrees to maintain appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards as required under SEC Regulation S-P. Provider shall not access, use, or disclose Client data except as necessary to perform the services described herein. Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

## 8. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive **twelve (12) month** terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the current term. Renewal pricing shall be at Provider's then-current published rates for the VaultLLM Basic tier. Provider shall notify Client of any rate changes at least forty-five (45) days prior to the renewal date.

## 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause with **thirty (30) days** written notice upon material breach that remains uncured within that period.
- Client may terminate for convenience at any time with **thirty (30) days** written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to **two (2) months** of the monthly subscription fee.
- Upon termination, Provider shall make Client's encrypted data available for export for a period of thirty (30) days. Following the export window, all Client data shall be securely deleted from Provider's systems, with written confirmation provided to Client within fifteen (15) days of deletion.
