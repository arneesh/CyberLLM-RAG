# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Trident Logistics — NetLLM Professional

**Document ID:** CL-NLM-2024-0734  
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2024  
**Prepared By:** CyberLLM Commercial Sales

---

## 1. Parties

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cyber Ridge Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Trident Logistics**, a Georgia limited liability company headquartered at 8800 Freight Corridor Drive, Atlanta, GA 30301 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Effective Date

- **Effective Date:** June 1, 2024
- **Initial Term:** Twenty-four (24) months, concluding May 31, 2026
- **Contract Value (Initial Term):** $216,000

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the **NetLLM Professional** platform configured for supply chain and logistics network environments, including:

- Continuous network security monitoring across Client's hub-and-spoke warehouse network (up to 25 monitored nodes)
- IoT device discovery and visibility engine covering fleet GPS trackers, warehouse automation sensors, RFID readers, and environmental controls
- AI-driven anomaly detection optimized for operational technology (OT) traffic patterns common in logistics environments
- API-based integration with Client's SAP ERP system for correlated security event analysis alongside shipment and inventory data
- Automated alerting for unauthorized lateral movement between IT and OT network segments
- Weekly automated threat summary reports and monthly executive dashboards
- Compliance mapping to NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) core functions

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Platform Fee | $9,000 |
| One-Time Setup & Integration Fee | $15,000 |

- Invoices are issued on the first business day of each calendar month and are payable within **thirty (30) days** of the invoice date.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at a rate of **1.5% per month** or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less.
- The one-time setup fee is due upon execution and covers initial deployment, ERP API integration, and IoT device onboarding.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.9% measured monthly
- **Critical Incident Response:** Within thirty (30) minutes, 24/7/365
- **High-Severity Incident Response:** Within two (2) hours
- **Standard Incident Response:** Within eight (8) business hours
- **SLA Credit:** For each full 0.1% below the 99.9% uptime commitment, Client shall receive a credit equal to 3% of the applicable monthly fee, capped at 20%.

## 6. Support

- **Tier:** Professional Support
- **Named Contact:** Assigned Technical Account Manager available during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM ET)
- **Support Channels:** Email ticketing system, phone support during business hours, emergency after-hours hotline
- **Quarterly Business Reviews:** Provider shall conduct quarterly reviews covering threat landscape, platform performance, and optimization recommendations

## 7. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to hold in confidence all non-public information disclosed by the other party during the term of this Agreement, including but not limited to network architectures, supply chain routing data, security configurations, and proprietary algorithms. The obligations of this section shall survive termination of the Agreement for a period of three (3) years. Both parties shall limit access to Confidential Information to personnel with a legitimate need to know.

## 8. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive **twelve (12) month** periods at the then-current list pricing for the NetLLM Professional tier unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **sixty (60) days** prior to the end of the current term. Renewal rate increases shall not exceed **5%** annually.

## 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon **forty-five (45) days** written notice, provided the breaching party fails to cure within that period.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon **ninety (90) days** written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to **three (3) months** of the monthly platform fee.
- Upon termination or expiration, Provider shall export all Client data in a standard format within thirty (30) days and securely delete all copies within sixty (60) days, upon written request.
