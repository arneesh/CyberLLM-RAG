# Master SaaS Services Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Apex Investment Group — CompliLLM Platform

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025
**Contract Number:** CL-AIG-2025-0018

---

## 1. Parties

- **Provider:** CyberLLM Inc., a Delaware corporation, with principal offices at 440 Security Boulevard, Suite 1200, Austin, TX 78701 ("CyberLLM").
- **Client:** Apex Investment Group, a New York corporation and registered financial services holding company, with principal offices at 1 Park Avenue, 38th Floor, New York, NY 10016 ("Client").

## 2. Term and Effective Date

This Agreement shall commence on January 15, 2025 and continue for an initial term of thirty-six (36) months, expiring on January 14, 2028 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8.

## 3. Subscription Tier and Monthly Fee

- **Tier:** CompliLLM Enterprise (Custom)
- **Monthly Subscription Fee:** $16,000.00 USD
- **One-Time Implementation and Integration Fee:** $45,000.00 USD (invoiced in two installments)
- **Licensed Entities:** Up to eight (8) subsidiary entities under the Apex Investment Group corporate umbrella

## 4. Scope of Services and Features

CyberLLM shall provide Client access to the CompliLLM Enterprise platform with the following custom capabilities:

- Sarbanes-Oxley (SOX) Section 302 and Section 404 automated compliance workflows, including control documentation, testing schedules, and management certification tracking.
- PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance automation for subsidiaries processing cardholder data, with quarterly evidence collection and gap remediation tracking.
- SOC 2 Type II continuous monitoring with automated evidence gathering across all eight licensed entities.
- Unified compliance posture dashboard providing holding-company-level aggregation with entity-level drill-down.
- Continuous monitoring engine with real-time alerting for compliance drift across all frameworks.
- Cross-entity policy harmonization tools for consistent governance across subsidiaries operating under different regulatory regimes.
- Custom regulatory change management feed covering SEC, FINRA, OCC, and CFPB regulatory updates with automated impact assessments.
- API integrations with Client's existing GRC platform (ServiceNow GRC) and internal audit management system.
- Dedicated compliance data lake with 7-year retention in accordance with SEC record-keeping requirements.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** CyberLLM guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime, excluding pre-approved maintenance windows.
- **Incident Response:** Critical incidents shall receive initial response within thirty (30) minutes, 24/7/365.
- **Escalation Path:** A named Technical Account Manager (TAM) shall serve as the single point of escalation for all SLA-related matters.
- **SLA Credits:** For each 0.01% below the 99.95% threshold, Client shall receive a credit of 3% of that month's fee, capped at 30%.

## 6. Payment Terms

- Monthly subscription fees are invoiced on the first business day of each calendar month and payable within forty-five (45) days via wire transfer.
- The implementation fee shall be invoiced as follows: 50% upon contract execution and 50% upon completion of the onboarding phase (estimated 60 days).
- Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Invoices disputed in good faith shall not accrue interest while under review, provided Client notifies CyberLLM in writing within fifteen (15) days of invoice receipt.

## 7. Support

- **Support Tier:** Enterprise Premium (24/7)
- **Channels:** Dedicated Slack channel, priority phone line, in-platform ticketing, and email.
- **Response Times:** Critical within 30 minutes; high within 2 hours; medium within 4 hours; low within 1 business day.
- **Technical Account Manager:** A dedicated TAM shall be assigned, available for weekly syncs and quarterly executive business reviews.
- **Training:** Unlimited remote training sessions during the onboarding phase; up to four (4) on-site training days per year.

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause upon sixty (60) days' written notice following an uncured material breach, provided the breaching party has been given thirty (30) days to cure.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon one hundred twenty (120) days' written notice, subject to payment of an early termination fee equal to six (6) months of the then-current subscription fee.
- Upon termination, CyberLLM shall assist with data migration at Client's reasonable request, at CyberLLM's then-current professional services rates.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twenty-four (24) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term. Renewal pricing shall be subject to negotiation, with increases capped at the greater of 4% or CPI-U.

## 10. Confidentiality and Data Protection

All non-public financial data, audit findings, and compliance records shared under this Agreement constitute Confidential Information. CyberLLM shall maintain all Client data in U.S.-based, SOC 2 Type II-certified data centers with encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). CyberLLM shall notify Client of any security incident affecting Client data within twenty-four (24) hours of confirmed detection.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Neither party's aggregate liability shall exceed the total fees paid or payable during the twenty-four (24) month period preceding the claim. This limitation shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality, data protection obligations, or willful misconduct.
