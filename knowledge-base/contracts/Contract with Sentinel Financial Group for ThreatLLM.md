# Master Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Sentinel Financial Group — ThreatLLM Enterprise

**Agreement Number:** CL-TLM-2024-0091
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024
**Document Classification:** Confidential

---

## 1. Parties

This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Sentinel Financial Group**, a New York corporation with principal offices at 200 Wall Street, New York, NY 10005 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Duration

This Agreement shall commence on January 15, 2024, and shall continue for a period of **thirty-six (36) months**, terminating on January 14, 2027, unless renewed or terminated in accordance with the provisions herein.

## 3. Product and Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ThreatLLM Enterprise** — an AI-powered threat intelligence and detection platform — configured for financial services environments. The scope includes:

- Real-time threat intelligence feeds curated for the financial sector, including banking trojans, payment fraud campaigns, and nation-state APT tracking
- Dark web monitoring for Sentinel Financial Group-specific indicators of compromise (IOCs), leaked credentials, and brand impersonation
- Full MITRE ATT&CK framework integration with automated technique mapping across all detected threats
- Dedicated threat feed with custom detection rules aligned to Sentinel's asset inventory and risk profile
- AI-driven behavioral analysis engine processing up to 50 million events per day
- Quarterly threat landscape reports authored by CyberLLM's Threat Research Division
- Integration with Client's existing Splunk SIEM and ServiceNow ITSM platforms

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Platform Fee | $15,000.00 |
| One-Time Setup & Integration Fee | $45,000.00 |
| Annual Threat Assessment Report | Included |

- All fees are billed monthly in arrears. Invoices are issued on the 1st of each calendar month.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less.
- The one-time setup fee shall be invoiced upon execution of this Agreement and is due within forty-five (45) days.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

Provider guarantees the following service levels:

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.95% measured monthly, excluding scheduled maintenance windows
- **Threat Feed Latency:** New IOCs published within 15 minutes of validation
- **Critical Alert Response:** Acknowledged within 15 minutes, initial analysis within 1 hour
- **SLA Credits:** For each 0.1% below the uptime guarantee, Client shall receive a credit of 5% of that month's fee, up to a maximum of 30%

## 6. Support

Client shall receive **Tier 1 — Dedicated Enterprise Support**, which includes:

- 24/7/365 access to a named Threat Analyst assigned exclusively to Sentinel Financial Group
- Dedicated Slack channel and encrypted communications portal
- Bi-weekly threat briefing calls with CyberLLM's Senior Threat Intelligence team
- Escalation to CyberLLM's Principal Threat Researchers within 2 hours for critical incidents
- On-site support available upon request (up to 4 visits per year, travel expenses billed separately)

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information exchanged under this Agreement. Confidential information includes, but is not limited to, threat intelligence data, detection methodologies, network architecture details, and financial terms. This obligation survives termination for a period of five (5) years.

## 8. Data Handling and Compliance

- All Client data shall be encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
- Provider shall comply with SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and NIST Cybersecurity Framework requirements
- Provider acknowledges Client's obligations under GLBA, PCI-DSS, and SEC/FINRA regulations and shall support compliance efforts
- Annual third-party penetration testing results shall be made available to Client upon request

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing shall not increase by more than 5% annually without mutual written agreement.

## 10. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon sixty (60) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision and fails to cure such breach within the notice period.
- Client may terminate for convenience with one hundred twenty (120) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to 50% of the remaining monthly fees for the unexpired term.
- Upon termination, Provider shall securely destroy or return all Client data within thirty (30) days and provide written certification of destruction.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.
