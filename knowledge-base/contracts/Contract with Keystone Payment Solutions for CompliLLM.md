# SaaS Subscription Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Keystone Payment Solutions — CompliLLM Platform

**Effective Date:** June 1, 2025
**Contract Number:** CL-KPS-2025-0091

---

## 1. Parties

- **Provider:** CyberLLM Inc., a Delaware corporation, with principal offices at 440 Security Boulevard, Suite 1200, Austin, TX 78701 ("CyberLLM").
- **Client:** Keystone Payment Solutions, Inc., a Georgia corporation and registered payment processor, with principal offices at 750 Peachtree Street NE, Suite 600, Atlanta, GA 30308 ("Client").

## 2. Term and Effective Date

This Agreement shall become effective on June 1, 2025 and shall remain in force for an initial term of eighteen (18) months, expiring on November 30, 2026 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated pursuant to Section 8.

## 3. Subscription Tier and Monthly Fee

- **Tier:** CompliLLM Professional
- **Monthly Subscription Fee:** $8,000.00 USD
- **One-Time Setup Fee:** $10,000.00 USD (due upon execution)

## 4. Scope of Services and Features

CyberLLM shall provide Client access to the CompliLLM Professional platform, including:

- PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance automation covering all twelve (12) requirement families, with particular emphasis on Requirement 6 (Secure Systems), Requirement 10 (Logging and Monitoring), and Requirement 11 (Security Testing).
- Quarterly Approved Scanning Vendor (ASV) scanning integration with automated ingestion of scan results and remediation tracking workflows.
- Real-time compliance dashboards displaying current posture against PCI-DSS v4.0 controls with customizable risk heat maps.
- Automated Self-Assessment Questionnaire (SAQ) generation and Report on Compliance (ROC) preparation tools.
- Evidence collection automation for annual QSA audits, including screenshot capture, log aggregation, and configuration snapshots.
- Cardholder data environment (CDE) scope management tools with network segmentation validation.
- Vendor risk management module for tracking third-party service provider PCI compliance status.
- Automated alerting for compliance drift, with configurable escalation paths to compliance officers.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** CyberLLM guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for the CompliLLM platform.
- **Scheduled Maintenance:** Maintenance windows shall be scheduled outside of Client's peak processing hours (Saturday 2:00 AM–6:00 AM ET) with at least 48 hours' advance notice.
- **Incident Response:** Critical platform issues shall receive initial response within one (1) hour, 24/7.
- **SLA Credits:** For each full hour below the 99.9% threshold, Client shall receive a service credit of 2% of the monthly fee, capped at 25%.

## 6. Payment Terms

- Subscription fees shall be invoiced monthly on the first business day of each month.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date via ACH or wire transfer.
- Late payments shall incur a finance charge of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance.
- The setup fee shall be invoiced at contract execution and is non-refundable regardless of early termination.

## 7. Support

- **Support Tier:** Priority Support (Professional tier)
- **Channels:** Email, phone, and in-platform ticketing.
- **Response Times:** Critical issues within 1 hour (24/7); high-priority within 4 hours (business hours); standard within 1 business day.
- **QSA Liaison:** CyberLLM shall designate a compliance specialist to serve as a liaison during Client's annual PCI-DSS audit.
- **Training:** One (1) onboarding training session and one (1) refresher session at the six-month mark.

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause upon forty-five (45) days' written notice if the other party commits a material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days after written notice.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to the lesser of (a) three (3) months of subscription fees or (b) the remaining balance of the Initial Term.
- CyberLLM reserves the right to terminate immediately if Client's use of the platform violates applicable law or poses a security risk to other customers.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least forty-five (45) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Fees upon renewal may increase by up to 5% with sixty (60) days' prior written notice.

## 10. Confidentiality

Each party shall treat as confidential all non-public information received from the other party. CyberLLM acknowledges that Client data may include cardholder data subject to PCI-DSS and agrees to maintain all such data in PCI-DSS Level 1 certified infrastructure. CyberLLM shall not store, process, or transmit primary account numbers (PANs) in cleartext at any time.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Except for indemnification obligations and breaches of data protection provisions, neither party's aggregate liability shall exceed the total fees paid or payable under this Agreement during the twelve (12) months preceding the event giving rise to such liability.
