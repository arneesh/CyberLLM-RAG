# SaaS Services Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and MedFirst Health Network — CompliLLM Platform

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2025
**Contract Number:** CL-MFH-2025-0042

---

## 1. Parties

- **Provider:** CyberLLM Inc., a Delaware corporation, with principal offices at 440 Security Boulevard, Suite 1200, Austin, TX 78701 ("CyberLLM").
- **Client:** MedFirst Health Network, a California nonprofit health system, with principal offices at 2100 Pacific Medical Drive, San Diego, CA 92103 ("Client").

## 2. Term and Effective Date

This Agreement shall commence on March 1, 2025 and continue for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months, expiring on February 28, 2027 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8.

## 3. Subscription Tier and Monthly Fee

- **Tier:** CompliLLM Professional
- **Monthly Subscription Fee:** $8,000.00 USD
- **One-Time Setup and Onboarding Fee:** $12,000.00 USD (due upon execution)
- **Licensed Facilities:** Up to fifteen (15) healthcare facilities under MedFirst Health Network

## 4. Scope of Services and Features

CyberLLM shall provide Client access to the CompliLLM Professional platform, which includes:

- Automated compliance monitoring and enforcement for HIPAA Privacy Rule, HIPAA Security Rule, and HITECH Act requirements across all fifteen licensed facilities.
- SOC 2 Type II audit preparation toolkit with continuous control monitoring and evidence collection.
- Audit-ready report generation with exportable artifacts formatted for OCR, HHS, and state regulatory bodies.
- Real-time compliance dashboards with facility-level drill-down and executive summary views.
- Policy lifecycle management including version control, attestation tracking, and automated distribution.
- Risk assessment engine with quarterly automated risk scoring aligned to NIST Cybersecurity Framework.
- Integration connectors for Epic EHR, Cerner, and Meditech systems for access-log compliance monitoring.
- Breach notification workflow automation in accordance with HHS 60-day notification requirements.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** CyberLLM guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime for the CompliLLM platform, excluding scheduled maintenance windows communicated at least 72 hours in advance.
- **Incident Response:** Critical compliance platform incidents will receive initial response within one (1) hour during business hours (8:00 AM–8:00 PM CT, Monday–Friday).
- **SLA Credits:** For each full hour of downtime below the 99.9% threshold in a calendar month, Client shall receive a credit equal to 2% of that month's subscription fee, up to a maximum of 20%.

## 6. Payment Terms

- Monthly subscription fees are billed on the first business day of each calendar month.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date via ACH, wire transfer, or corporate credit card.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.
- The one-time setup fee shall be invoiced upon contract execution and is non-refundable.

## 7. Support

- **Support Tier:** Priority Support (Professional tier)
- **Channels:** Dedicated email queue, phone support, and in-platform ticketing system.
- **Response Times:** Critical issues within 1 hour; high-priority within 4 hours; standard within 1 business day.
- **Dedicated CSM:** Client shall be assigned a named Customer Success Manager with quarterly business reviews.
- **Training:** Up to two (2) remote onboarding training sessions for compliance officers at each facility.

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate this Agreement for cause upon sixty (60) days' written notice if the other party materially breaches any provision and fails to cure within thirty (30) days.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon ninety (90) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to three (3) months of the then-current monthly subscription fee.
- Upon termination, CyberLLM shall provide Client with an export of all compliance data in machine-readable format within thirty (30) days.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing may be adjusted by up to 5% with ninety (90) days' advance written notice.

## 10. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to hold in confidence all Confidential Information of the other party. Given the nature of protected health information (PHI) handled under this Agreement, the parties shall execute a separate Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as required under HIPAA. CyberLLM shall maintain all Client data in SOC 2-certified, HIPAA-compliant data centers within the continental United States.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Except for breaches of confidentiality or data protection obligations, neither party's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall exceed the total fees paid or payable during the twelve (12) month period preceding the event giving rise to the claim.
