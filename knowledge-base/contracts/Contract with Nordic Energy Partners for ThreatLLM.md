# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Nordic Energy Partners — ThreatLLM Professional

**Agreement Number:** CL-TLM-2024-0319
**Effective Date:** August 1, 2024
**Document Classification:** Confidential

---

## 1. Parties

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation, 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Nordic Energy Partners**, a Colorado corporation, 5500 Turbine Road, Denver, CO 80202 ("Client")

## 2. Term

Eighteen (18) months commencing August 1, 2024, and expiring January 31, 2026.

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ThreatLLM Professional** configured for critical infrastructure and energy sector environments:

- OT/ICS-specific threat intelligence covering threats to SCADA systems, PLCs, RTUs, and industrial control networks
- Tracking of threat actors known to target energy infrastructure (e.g., Sandworm, Volt Typhoon, XENOTIME)
- NERC CIP compliance alignment: threat data mapped to NERC CIP-005, CIP-007, and CIP-010 requirements
- Integration with Client's Fortinet SIEM and OT network monitoring tools via syslog and REST API
- Bi-weekly threat intelligence bulletins focused on energy sector campaigns, vulnerabilities in ICS/SCADA products, and geopolitical risk indicators
- Up to 8 custom detection rules per quarter aligned to Client's OT environment
- Access to CyberLLM's Critical Infrastructure Threat Portal with ICS-specific IOC feeds

## 4. Fees

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription Fee | $7,500.00 |
| One-Time OT Integration Fee | $20,000.00 |

- Monthly invoices on the 1st of each month; payment due net 30.
- Late payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.
- Integration fee invoiced upon execution; due within forty-five (45) days.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

- **Platform Availability:** 99.9% monthly uptime
- **OT Threat Alert Latency:** Critical ICS/OT-specific alerts delivered within 20 minutes of validation
- **Intelligence Update Frequency:** Minimum daily updates to ICS threat feeds
- **SLA Credits:** 10% of monthly fee for each 0.1% below the 99.9% target, capped at 30%

## 6. Support

Client receives **Tier 2 — Professional Support with OT Specialization**:

- Extended business hours: 6:00 AM – 10:00 PM MT, Monday–Saturday
- Critical OT-related issues: 30-minute response time, 24/7
- High severity: 2-hour response during extended business hours
- Named Customer Success Manager with monthly review calls
- Access to CyberLLM's OT/ICS Threat Research team for ad-hoc consultations (up to 4 hours per month)

## 7. Compliance and Regulatory

- Provider acknowledges Client's obligations under NERC CIP and TSA Security Directives for pipeline operators.
- Threat intelligence deliverables shall include compliance context mapping where applicable.
- Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II certification and undergoes annual third-party security assessments.
- Provider shall cooperate with NERC audits or TSA inspections relevant to threat intelligence services as reasonably requested.

## 8. Confidentiality

All proprietary and operational information exchanged under this Agreement is confidential. Neither party shall disclose such information without written consent except where required by regulatory authority or law. Energy sector operational data (grid topology, SCADA configurations, etc.) shall receive enhanced protection and restricted access within Provider's organization. Confidentiality obligations survive termination for four (4) years.

## 9. Renewal

Auto-renews for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal. Renewal pricing subject to a maximum annual increase of 5%.

## 10. Termination

- **For Cause:** Either party may terminate upon sixty (60) days' written notice for material breach remaining uncured.
- **For Convenience:** Client may terminate with ninety (90) days' notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to three (3) months of subscription fees.
- **Regulatory:** If a regulatory order materially prevents Client from using the service, Client may terminate without penalty upon providing evidence of such order.
- Upon termination, Provider shall securely delete all Client data within twenty (20) business days and provide written certification.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Provider's total aggregate liability under this Agreement is limited to fees paid by Client during the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Neither party is liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages except where arising from breach of confidentiality or gross negligence.
