# SaaS Services Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Nordic Telecom AB — CompliLLM Platform

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2025
**Contract Number:** CL-NTA-2025-0067

---

## 1. Parties

- **Provider:** CyberLLM Inc., a Delaware corporation, with principal offices at 440 Security Boulevard, Suite 1200, Austin, TX 78701 ("CyberLLM").
- **Client:** Nordic Telecom AB, a Swedish aktiebolag (public limited company), with registered offices at Kungsgatan 44, SE-111 35 Stockholm, Sweden ("Client").

## 2. Term and Effective Date

This Agreement shall commence on April 1, 2025 and continue for an initial term of twenty-four (24) months, expiring on March 31, 2027 (the "Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8.

## 3. Subscription Tier and Monthly Fee

- **Tier:** CompliLLM Enterprise (Custom)
- **Monthly Subscription Fee:** $12,000.00 USD
- **One-Time Implementation Fee:** $30,000.00 USD (invoiced in two equal installments)
- **Licensed Jurisdictions:** Sweden, Norway, Finland, Denmark, and Germany

## 4. Scope of Services and Features

CyberLLM shall provide Client access to the CompliLLM Enterprise platform with the following capabilities:

- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance automation including data processing activity registers, Data Protection Impact Assessments (DPIAs), and Subject Access Request (SAR) workflow management.
- ISO 27001:2022 Information Security Management System (ISMS) support with control mapping, Statement of Applicability generation, and internal audit scheduling.
- NIS2 Directive compliance modules covering incident reporting obligations, supply chain risk management, and governance requirements for essential service operators in the telecommunications sector.
- Multi-country data processing compliance engine supporting divergent national implementations of EU directives across the five licensed jurisdictions.
- Cross-border data transfer compliance tools including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) management, Transfer Impact Assessments (TIAs), and Binding Corporate Rules (BCR) tracking.
- Regulatory change monitoring for ENISA advisories, national telecom authority (PTS, Nkom, Traficom, DDMA, BNetzA) publications, and EU legislative updates.
- Multi-language support for compliance documentation in English, Swedish, Norwegian, Finnish, Danish, and German.
- Integration with Client's existing ServiceNow ITSM and OneTrust privacy management platforms via REST API.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** CyberLLM guarantees 99.9% monthly uptime, measured across the EU-hosted instance.
- **Data Residency:** All Client data shall be processed and stored within the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany data center region).
- **Incident Response:** Critical incidents shall receive initial response within one (1) hour during extended business hours (7:00 AM–10:00 PM CET, Monday–Friday) and within four (4) hours on weekends.
- **SLA Credits:** For each 0.1% below the 99.9% uptime threshold, Client receives a 5% credit on the monthly fee, capped at 25%.

## 6. Payment Terms

- Subscription fees are invoiced monthly in USD on the first business day of each month. Client may remit payment in EUR at the ECB reference rate on the date of payment.
- Payment is due within thirty (30) days of invoice date via SEPA bank transfer or international wire.
- Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate permitted under Swedish commercial law (Räntelagen), currently the Riksbank reference rate plus 8 percentage points.
- The implementation fee shall be invoiced 50% at execution and 50% upon completion of onboarding.

## 7. Support

- **Support Tier:** Enterprise Support
- **Channels:** Priority email, phone, in-platform ticketing, and dedicated Microsoft Teams channel.
- **Response Times:** Critical within 1 hour; high within 4 hours; medium within 8 hours; low within 2 business days.
- **Dedicated Resources:** A named Customer Success Manager and a Compliance Solutions Architect shall be assigned to the account.
- **Training:** Up to three (3) remote training sessions per jurisdiction during the onboarding phase, plus annual refresher workshops.

## 8. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause upon sixty (60) days' written notice following a material breach that remains uncured for thirty (30) days.
- Client may terminate for convenience upon ninety (90) days' written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to four (4) months of the then-current subscription fee.
- In the event of termination, CyberLLM shall provide a complete data export in structured format (JSON/CSV) within forty-five (45) days and shall certify deletion of all Client data within ninety (90) days, in compliance with GDPR Article 17.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing adjustments shall be communicated at least sixty (60) days in advance and shall not exceed 5% per annum.

## 10. Confidentiality and Data Protection

This Agreement is subject to a separate Data Processing Agreement (DPA) executed between the parties, in which CyberLLM acts as data processor on behalf of Client as data controller. CyberLLM shall comply with all applicable provisions of the GDPR, including Article 28 processor obligations. All personal data shall remain within the EU at all times. CyberLLM shall maintain ISO 27001 certification for its EU operations throughout the term.

## 11. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Sweden, without regard to conflict of laws principles. Disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under the rules of the Stockholm Chamber of Commerce (SCC).
