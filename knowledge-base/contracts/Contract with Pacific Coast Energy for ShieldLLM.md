# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Pacific Coast Energy — ShieldLLM Business

**Agreement Number:** CL-SLM-2024-0178
**Effective Date:** April 15, 2024
**Document Classification:** Confidential

---

## 1. Parties

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Pacific Coast Energy**, 3200 Grid Avenue, Portland, OR 97204 ("Client")

## 2. Term

Twenty-four (24) months, from April 15, 2024, through April 14, 2026.

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ShieldLLM Business** — AI-powered endpoint detection and response — configured for energy sector IT environments:

- Licensed for up to **3,500 endpoints** across Client's corporate IT infrastructure
- AI-driven behavioral detection, automated threat containment, and endpoint forensics
- SCADA-adjacent endpoint monitoring: ShieldLLM agents deployed on IT endpoints within the SCADA demilitarized zone (DMZ) and data historian servers to detect threats attempting to pivot from IT to OT networks
- Real-time threat dashboard with energy sector contextualization
- Integration with Client's Microsoft Sentinel SIEM via native connector
- Automated weekly endpoint health reports identifying unprotected or degraded agents
- Quarterly vulnerability correlation: mapping endpoint vulnerabilities to known energy sector threat campaigns

## 4. Fees

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription (3,500 endpoints) | $6,000.00 |
| One-Time Setup & Integration Fee | $12,000.00 |
| Additional Endpoints (beyond 3,500) | $2.00/endpoint/month |

- Invoices on the 1st of each month; net 30 payment terms.
- Late payments subject to 1.5% monthly interest.
- Setup fee due upon execution.
- Endpoint counts reviewed semi-annually.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

- **Agent Reporting Uptime:** 99.8% of deployed agents reporting to console, measured monthly
- **Management Console Availability:** 99.9% monthly
- **Detection to Alert Latency:** Under 90 seconds for behavioral detections
- **SLA Credit:** 10% of monthly fee if agent uptime falls below 99.8%; max credit 20%

## 6. Support

Client receives **Tier 2 — Business Support**:

- Extended business hours: 7:00 AM – 9:00 PM PT, Monday–Friday
- Critical severity: 1-hour response, 24/7
- High severity: 4-hour response during business hours
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager
- Monthly operational review calls
- Access to CyberLLM Knowledge Base and community forums

## 7. SCADA-Adjacent Monitoring Provisions

- ShieldLLM agents deployed in SCADA-adjacent zones shall operate in detect-only mode. No automated containment actions will be executed on these endpoints without explicit Client authorization.
- Provider shall maintain documentation of all SCADA-adjacent agent configurations and make it available to Client for regulatory compliance purposes.
- Provider acknowledges Client's NERC CIP obligations and shall support Client's evidence collection for CIP-007 (System Security Management) as it relates to endpoint protection.

## 8. Confidentiality

Confidential information exchanged under this Agreement shall not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent. This includes all endpoint telemetry, detection data, network architecture information, and commercial terms. Obligation survives for three (3) years post-termination.

## 9. Renewal

Auto-renews for twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice before expiration. Price adjustments capped at 5% annually.

## 10. Termination

- **For Cause:** Sixty (60) days' written notice for material uncured breach.
- **For Convenience:** Ninety (90) days' notice from Client, subject to early termination fee of three (3) months' subscription.
- Provider shall uninstall all agents and delete Client data within thirty (30) days of termination, with written confirmation provided.

## 11. Liability

Provider's maximum aggregate liability shall not exceed fees paid in the preceding twelve (12) months. Neither party liable for indirect or consequential damages.
