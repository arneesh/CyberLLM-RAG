# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Meridian Healthcare Systems — ThreatLLM Professional

**Agreement Number:** CL-TLM-2024-0147
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Document Classification:** Confidential

---

## 1. Parties

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Meridian Healthcare Systems**, a California corporation with principal offices at 1200 Caduceus Drive, San Diego, CA 92101 ("Client")

## 2. Term

This Agreement shall commence on March 1, 2024, and remain in effect for **twenty-four (24) months**, expiring on February 28, 2026.

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ThreatLLM Professional**, an AI-powered threat intelligence platform tailored for healthcare environments:

- Healthcare-specific threat intelligence covering ransomware groups targeting hospitals, medical device vulnerabilities, and PHI-targeting campaigns
- HIPAA-compliant data handling with all threat data processed in Provider's FedRAMP-authorized infrastructure
- Integration with Client's existing IBM QRadar SIEM via pre-built connectors and API access
- Automated IOC enrichment with healthcare sector context (HHS alerts, FDA device advisories, HC3 bulletins)
- Weekly threat digests summarizing relevant campaigns and recommended mitigations
- Up to 10 custom detection rules per quarter, tuned for Client's environment

## 4. Fees

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription Fee | $7,500.00 |
| One-Time Integration Fee | $15,000.00 |

- Invoices issued on the 1st of each month; payment due within thirty (30) days.
- Late payments incur interest at 1.5% per month.
- Integration fee due upon contract execution.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

- **Platform Availability:** 99.9% monthly uptime
- **Threat Intelligence Updates:** Minimum twice daily; critical alerts within 30 minutes of validation
- **SLA Remedies:** If uptime falls below 99.9% in any calendar month, Client receives a 10% credit on that month's fee. Maximum credit: 25% per month.

## 6. Support

Client receives **Tier 2 — Professional Support**:

- Business hours support (8:00 AM – 8:00 PM ET, Monday–Friday)
- Critical severity issues: 1-hour response time, 24/7
- High severity issues: 4-hour response during business hours
- Dedicated Customer Success Manager with monthly check-in calls
- Access to CyberLLM's Healthcare Threat Intelligence Portal

## 7. HIPAA and Compliance

- Provider shall execute a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) as an addendum to this Agreement prior to any processing of Protected Health Information (PHI).
- Provider maintains SOC 2 Type II and HITRUST CSF certifications.
- All threat data involving Client systems shall be stored in HIPAA-compliant, U.S.-based data centers.
- Provider shall notify Client of any security incident involving Client data within twenty-four (24) hours of discovery.

## 8. Confidentiality

All information exchanged under this Agreement shall be treated as confidential. Neither party shall disclose such information to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law or regulation. This obligation survives termination for three (3) years.

## 9. Renewal

This Agreement auto-renews for successive twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides sixty (60) days' written notice of non-renewal before the end of the current term.

## 10. Termination

- **For Cause:** Either party may terminate upon forty-five (45) days' written notice for material breach that remains uncured.
- **For Convenience:** Client may terminate with ninety (90) days' notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to three (3) months of subscription fees.
- **Data Return:** Upon termination, Provider shall return or destroy all Client data within fifteen (15) business days.

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Provider's total liability shall not exceed the fees paid by Client in the six (6) months prior to the event giving rise to the claim. Neither party shall be liable for consequential or indirect damages, except in cases of gross negligence or willful misconduct.
