# Service Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. & Granite State University — ShieldLLM Starter

**Agreement Number:** CL-SLM-2024-0301
**Effective Date:** July 1, 2024

---

## 1. Parties

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, 440 Cipher Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Granite State University**, 250 University Boulevard, Manchester, NH 03101 ("Client")

## 2. Term

Twelve (12) months, from July 1, 2024, through June 30, 2025.

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver **ShieldLLM Starter** — AI-powered endpoint detection and response:

- Licensed for up to **400 endpoints** (Windows and macOS)
- Core EDR capabilities: malware detection, ransomware prevention, and endpoint isolation
- Centralized management console for Client's IT security team
- Pre-configured detection policies optimized for education environments (lab workstations, faculty devices, administrative systems)
- Monthly endpoint protection summary reports
- Integration with Client's existing Microsoft 365 environment for identity-correlated alerting

## 4. Fees

| Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription (up to 400 endpoints) | $2,500.00 |
| Setup Fee | $3,500.00 |

- Monthly invoices on the 1st; payment due net 30.
- Late payments subject to 1.5% monthly interest.
- Setup fee invoiced upon execution.

## 5. Service Level Agreement

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.5% monthly
- **Detection Performance:** Alerts generated within 2 minutes of behavioral detection
- **SLA Credits:** If uptime falls below 99.5%, Client receives a 10% credit on that month's fee, up to 15% maximum

## 6. Support

Client receives **Tier 3 — Standard Support**:

- Business hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM ET, Monday–Friday
- Critical issues: 4-hour response
- All other issues: next business day
- Email and web portal support
- Quarterly check-in calls with Customer Success

## 7. Acceptable Use

Client is responsible for ensuring ShieldLLM agents are deployed only on university-owned or university-managed devices. Provider is not liable for issues arising from deployment on personal student devices not under Client's device management.

## 8. Confidentiality

Both parties shall protect confidential information. Provider shall not access or use endpoint data for purposes other than delivering contracted services. Obligation survives two (2) years post-termination.

## 9. FERPA Awareness

Provider acknowledges that Client's systems may contain student education records subject to the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). Provider shall not access, store, or process student education records. Endpoint telemetry collected by ShieldLLM shall be limited to security-relevant metadata and shall not include file contents or user-generated data.

## 10. Renewal

Auto-renews for twelve (12) month terms unless either party provides thirty (30) days' written notice of non-renewal.

## 11. Termination

- **For Cause:** Thirty (30) days' written notice for material uncured breach.
- **For Convenience:** Client may terminate with sixty (60) days' notice. Early termination fee: one (1) month's subscription.
- Provider removes agents and deletes data within thirty (30) days of termination.
