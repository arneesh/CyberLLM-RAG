# Service Subscription Agreement: CyberLLM Inc. and Coastal Media Group — NetLLM Standard

**Document ID:** CL-NLM-2024-1102  
**Effective Date:** November 15, 2024  
**Prepared By:** CyberLLM SMB Sales

---

## 1. Parties

This Service Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

- **CyberLLM Inc.**, a Delaware corporation with principal offices at 440 Cyber Ridge Boulevard, Austin, TX 78701 ("Provider")
- **Coastal Media Group**, a California corporation headquartered at 1250 Pacific Coast Highway, Suite 400, Los Angeles, CA 90045 ("Client")

## 2. Term and Effective Date

- **Effective Date:** November 15, 2024
- **Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, concluding November 14, 2025
- **Contract Value (Initial Term):** $48,000

## 3. Scope of Services

Provider shall deliver the **NetLLM Standard** platform tailored for digital media operations, including:

- Network security monitoring for up to 10 monitored network segments
- AI-enhanced DDoS detection and mitigation alerting, calibrated for high-bandwidth content streaming environments
- Content delivery network (CDN) traffic analysis and anomaly detection across Client's primary and secondary CDN providers
- Basic SIEM functionality with centralized log collection, search, and 90-day log retention
- Pre-built correlation rules for common media industry threats including content scraping, credential stuffing, and ad fraud traffic patterns
- Monthly automated security posture reports delivered via the Provider's web portal
- Integration with up to two (2) existing security tools via standard syslog or REST API

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

| Fee Component | Amount |
|---|---|
| Monthly Subscription Fee | $4,000 |
| One-Time Onboarding Fee | $5,000 |

- Invoices are generated on the first of each month and are due within **thirty (30) days**.
- Failure to remit payment within the due period will result in a late fee of **1.0% per month** on the outstanding balance.
- The onboarding fee covers initial platform configuration, CDN integration setup, and a two-day virtual training session for Client's IT staff.

## 5. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.5% measured on a monthly basis
- **Critical Alert Response:** Within one (1) hour during business hours (Mon–Fri, 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM PT)
- **Standard Alert Response:** Within one (1) business day
- **After-Hours Critical Response:** Best-effort basis, typically within four (4) hours
- **SLA Credit:** If uptime falls below 99.5% in a given month, Client is entitled to a credit of 10% of that month's subscription fee.

## 6. Support

- **Tier:** Standard Support
- **Support Channels:** Email-based ticketing system with web portal access
- **Phone Support:** Available during business hours; no dedicated account manager at this tier
- **Knowledge Base:** Full access to Provider's self-service documentation, how-to guides, and community forums
- **Training:** Initial onboarding training included; additional training sessions available at $1,500 per half-day

## 7. Confidentiality

Both parties shall treat as confidential any proprietary or non-public information received from the other party in connection with this Agreement. Client's content distribution strategies, audience analytics, and network infrastructure details shall be considered Confidential Information. Provider shall not disclose such information to third parties without Client's prior written consent, except as required by law. These obligations shall survive for two (2) years following termination.

## 8. Renewal

This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive **twelve (12) month** terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the expiration of the current term. Renewal pricing shall be at Provider's then-current published rates for the NetLLM Standard tier.

## 9. Termination

- Either party may terminate for cause with **thirty (30) days** written notice upon material breach that remains uncured.
- Client may terminate for convenience at any time with **sixty (60) days** written notice, subject to an early termination fee equal to **two (2) months** of the monthly subscription fee.
- Upon termination, Client's access to the platform will be suspended, and all stored log data will be available for export for thirty (30) days before permanent deletion.
